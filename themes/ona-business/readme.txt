== Description ==

Ona Business is a full site editing WordPress theme based on the minimal design style.
Featuring modern, clean looks and great intuitive tools to customize your website experience, Its modern design and versatile features make it the perfect choice for any type of small business or startup, allowing you to quickly create professional and stunning websites without any coding skills.
The theme provides users with features such as a customizable header and footer with drag-and-drop tools, fast page loading time, unlimited sidebars and blocks.
Demo: https://ona.deothemes.com/business
